History

The entire We 81 Chat history started back in August 2025, but before that, the two gd players PuffiballGD and Bruno Ziernik met up in MrtGamess's chat on TikTok and after that they started chatting. One day, the player We know as Symm. or Symmetrics joined the vc where the two were chatting, in Mrtgamess Community, after that they became good friends, then Puufy joined vc one time and everyone loved him. The last person that joined the group was Retep, a good friend of Symm. even though he wasn't really liked by the rest of the group, they got used to him and they added Retep to the group chat, That's how everything started. After the group was created, they went on onto making content, for example the We 81 Lore album, We 81 plays Mc, their gd creation rotation levels (like Buborek or No tachii) and We 81 beats. They have allot of inside jokes that can be seen, sometimes, used in videos, They are still active to this day.
